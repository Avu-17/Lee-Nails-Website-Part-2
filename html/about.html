About Lee Nails

Lee Nails is a growing nail business based in Ku Gompo in Eastern Cape,founded by Lisa Dodo a twenty seven year old female Zimbabwean entrepreneur. She began her nail journey in 2023 and has continued to improve her skills and creativity over time.

What inspired Lisa to start

After noticing a gap in the market and the challenges people faced in finding reliable nail technicians,Lisa was inspired to start her business to meet those needs.She offers a variety of nail styles including short,medium and long sets tailored to each clients preferences.Lisa is passionate about delivering neat and high quality results that leaves clients feeling happy and satisfied.
